This website is intended to be a guide for people looking to build a computer. It can either be a guide for new builders, a reference for experienced builders, or a helpful tool for any level of PC building. There is also a section dedicated to the web page that contains links to parts as well as websites where you can build a parts list for your pc and even have it ordered. The nab bar to the left performs multiple functions:
  • Home Page: Brings you back to this home page
  • How to Build a PC: A PC building guide step by step
  • Helpful Links: Links to YouTube videos regarding building steps
  • Frequently Asked Questions: FAQ's that PC builders have
  • Parts Recommendations: Parts that we recommend
  • Gaming Competitions: Information about Gaming Competitions

Refer to these links to be guided through your PC building process, or if you have any questions.
